Redwater Incident

Redwater was built underground to hide experiments. Officially a physics lab, unofficially it pushed a particle accelerator past its limits. At 2:17 AM, the machine spiked. Distortions appeared in the corridors, twenty-eight scientists died, and anomalies escaped into the sewers. The only plan left: restart the accelerator and try to undo it all. If it fails, there may be nothing left.
