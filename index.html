What is Affinity?

What is Affinity?

Affinity is a horror game with two levels: The Complex and The Sewers, each with unique objectives to complete before progressing.

Game Features:

Expect suspenseful gameplay with an upcoming PvP mode and a shop offering cosmetics and bundles.

Servers:

There are five public servers and the option for private servers to play with friends.

Live Events:

Enjoy random live events that offer limited-time challenges and surprises!
