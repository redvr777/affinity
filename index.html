About the Game

Affinity is a psychological horror game where players navigate an eerie world filled with unsettling creatures and dangerous environments. The goal is to survive and uncover the dark secrets hidden within the game world. Players must stay vigilant, solve puzzles, and avoid terrifying encounters.
