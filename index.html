About Affinity

Affinity VR is a thrilling horror game set in a mysterious underground facility. Explore industrial corridors, uncover secrets, and survive the dangers lurking in the shadows. Every room is designed to test your wits and courage.

WARNING: This game contains intense horror elements

Levels

The Complex:

Navigate through the lab corridors, solve puzzles, and avoid traps in this high-tech environment.

The Sewers:

Dark and damp, the sewers hide secrets and dangers. Can you survive the shadows?
